About D.Watson Pharmacy

Trusted healthcare and beauty partner since 1975

Our Story

D.Watson is a Pakistani retail pharmacy chain founded in 1975 by brothers Zahid and Zafar Iqbal Bakhtawari in Islamabad.

The company was named in tribute to the English doctor, Dr. Watson, who helped Zafar Iqbal with an eye ailment that almost caused him to lose his sight. This act of kindness and medical expertise inspired the brothers to create a pharmacy that would serve the community with the same dedication and care.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, affordable, and high-quality healthcare and beauty products to every household in Pakistan. We believe that good health and self-care should be available to everyone, regardless of their location or economic status.

Our Values

Compassion

We care deeply about the health and wellbeing of our customers and their families.

Trust

For nearly 50 years, we've been a trusted name in healthcare across Pakistan.

Quality

We source only the finest products from trusted manufacturers and suppliers.

Community

We're committed to serving our local communities with personalized care and attention.

Our Journey

1975
Foundation

Zahid and Zafar Iqbal Bakhtawari founded D.Watson Pharmacy in Islamabad, starting with a single store dedicated to providing quality medicines and healthcare products.

1980s
Expansion

Expanded to multiple locations across Islamabad and Rawalpindi, becoming a household name in the twin cities.

2000s
Digital Innovation

Launched online presence and e-commerce platform, making healthcare accessible to customers nationwide.

Today
Modern Healthcare

Continuing to serve millions of customers with a comprehensive range of medicines, cosmetics, health devices, and wellness products.
